## Executive Summary

Kenneth A. Mendoza is an independent quantum computing innovator who has developed a novel approach to quantum error mitigation with the potential to accelerate practical quantum computing applications. His Hybrid Harmonic Quantum (H²Q) methodology, protected by two provisional patents filed in late 2024, demonstrates significant improvements in quantum algorithm reliability.
